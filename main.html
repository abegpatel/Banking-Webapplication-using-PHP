WELCOME TO GIET BANK

GIET Bank Ltd is the third largest of the private-sector banks in India offering a comprehensive suite of financial products. The bank has its head office in Mumbai and Registered office in Ahmedabad. It has 3304 branches, 14,003 ATMs, and nine international offices. The bank employs over 55,000 people and had a market capitalization of ₹1.28 trillion (US$20 billion) (as on March 31, 2017).[4] It offers the entire spectrum of financial services large and mid-size corporates, SME, and retail businesses. As of 30 Jun. 2016, 30.81% shares are owned by promoters & promoter group (United India Insurance Company Limited, Oriental Insurance Company Limited, National Insurance Company Limited, New India Assurance Company Ltd, GIC, LIC & UTI). Remaining 69.19% shares are owned by Mutual Funds Institutions, FIIs, Financial Institutions (banks), Insurance companies, corporate bodies & individual investors among others.

GIET Bank opened a branch in Colombo in October 2011, as a Licensed Commercial Bank supervised by the Central Bank of Sri Lanka. Also in 2011, GIET Bank opened a representative offices in Abu Dhabi. In 2011, GIET bank inaugurated GIET House, its new corporate office in Worli, Mumbai. In 2013, GIET Bank's subsidiary, GIET Bank UK commenced banking operations. GIET Bank UK has a branch in London. Deepika Padukone, a Mumbai Film Industry (a.k.a. Bollywood ) actress is the brand ambassador of GIET Bank. In 2015, GIET Bank opens its representative office in Dhaka. The bank has over 50,000 employees (as of 31 March 2016). The bank incurred ₹26.7 billion (US$410 million) on employee benefits during the FY 2012–13. The average age of an GIET Bank employee is 29 years. The attrition rate in GIET Bank is approx. 9% per year.
